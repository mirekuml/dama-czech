About Us

Who We Are

DAMA International, also known as the Data Management Association International, is a global not-for-profit organization dedicated to advancing concepts and practices related to data management and information resource management, founded in 1980 in Los Angeles.

DAMA Czech Republic is the local chapter, leading the way in advancing data management practices within the Czech Republic. We serve as the key facilitator for professionals in Data and Information Management.

Mission

To lead the way in advancing the practices and concepts of information and data management in the Czech Republic.

Vision

To be the key facilitator and essential organization for professionals in Data and Information Management in the Czech Republic.

Goals

Increase awareness of data management best practices. Promote adoption in Czech organizations. Foster a community of professionals.
